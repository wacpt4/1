口语关键句复习闪卡

我想说的短期工作是在德国做外卖骑手，因为这份工作对我来说非常合适。
英文表达：The short-term job I would like to talk about is working as a delivery rider in Germany, because it’s quite suitable for me.
关键概括：外卖兼职 (delivery rider)
一个原因是这类工作不需要复杂面试。
英文表达：One reason is that this kind of job doesn’t require a complicated interview.
关键概括：容易申请 (easy to get)
另一个原因是它可以帮助我减轻经济压力。
英文表达：Another reason is that it can help me relieve my financial pressure.
关键概括：财务压力 (financial pressure)
它可以支付我在德国的生活费用，甚至支持我在德国的旅行。
英文表达：It can cover my living expenses in Germany, or even support my exploration in Germany.
关键概括：生活开支 (living expenses)
去年夏天，我鼓励我的妹妹在暑假做一份临时工作。
英文表达：Last summer, I encouraged my young sister to do a temporary job during her summer holidays.
关键概括：鼓励 (encourage)
一开始她不愿意，但在我解释了好处之后，她接受了我的建议。
英文表达：At first, she was unwilling to do it, but after I explained the advantages, she accepted my advice.
关键概括：态度变化 (reaction to advice)
这份临时工作给她提供了提高德语并与当地人交流的机会。
英文表达：The temporary job offered her a chance to improve her German and interact with local people.
关键概括：语言与文化 (language & culture)
它还帮助她变得更加独立，并在旅行期间管理自己的财务。
英文表达：It also helped her become more independent and manage her finances during her trip.
关键概括：独立与理财 (independence & finances)
我第一次尝试的兴奋活动是在网上与外国人交流。
英文表达：The exciting activity I tried for the first time was communicating with a foreigner on an online platform.
关键概括：语言练习 (language practice)
在联系外国人之前，我感到紧张和羞愧，但最后我找到了一位和我一样有耐心的人。
英文表达：Before connecting with a foreigner, I felt nervous and ashamed, but finally I found someone patient like me.
关键概括：紧张/耐心 (nervous & patience)
我们克服了紧张，在一起练习语言技能时建立了友谊。
英文表达：We overcame our nervousness and developed a friendship while practicing language skills together.
关键概括：克服/友谊 (overcome & friendship)
我做了几个准备步骤，最后我能够定期和另一位外国人交流。
英文表达：I prepared for it in several steps, and finally I could speak with another person regularly.
关键概括：准备/定期练习 (preparation & regular practice)
这位外国人的语言水平和我相似，我们彼此耐心理解对方。
英文表达：The foreigner’s language level was similar to mine, and we were patient with each other to understand one another.
关键概括：耐心/理解 (patience & understanding)
我建议妹妹在德国做临时外卖工作，以支付她的生活和旅行费用。
英文表达：I suggested my sister do a temporary delivery job in Germany to cover her living and travel expenses.
关键概括：建议/经济 (advice & financial)
一开始她不愿意，但在比较其他选项后，她接受了我的建议。
英文表达：At first she was unwilling, but after comparing other options, she accepted my advice.
关键概括：态度变化 (reaction to advice)
这份工作不需要复杂面试，容易获得，这使它更适合她。
英文表达：This job was easy to get without a complicated interview, which made it more suitable for her.
关键概括：容易申请 (easy to get)
它还提供了练习德语、与当地人交流并获得宝贵经验的机会。
英文表达：It also offered her a chance to practice German, interact with locals, and gain valuable experience.
关键概括：语言/经验 (language & experience)
这份工作很灵活，她可以在任何城市工作，在旅行时按小时拿工资。
英文表达：It was a flexible job, so she could work in any city and earn an hourly wage while traveling.
关键概括：灵活性/收入 (flexibility & income)
她需要克服语言和文化障碍，而这份工作提供了练习德语的机会。
英文表达：She needed to overcome language and cultural barriers, and this job offered the opportunity to practice her German.
关键概括：语言/文化障碍 (language & cultural barriers)
最终，她变得更独立，管理自己的财务，并在工作期间探索德国城市。
英文表达：Ultimately, she became more independent, managed her finances, and explored German cities while working.
关键概括：独立/探索 (independence & exploration)
我认为领导可以通过耐心的方式来鼓励员工。
英文表达：I think leaders can encourage their employees in a patient way.
关键概括：领导鼓励 (leader & encouragement)
保持团队氛围很重要，这样每个成员才能朝同一个目标努力。
英文表达：Maintaining a good atmosphere is important, so that every member works toward the same goal.
关键概括：团队氛围 (team atmosphere)
这种做法可以帮助员工更好地完成上级分配的任务。
英文表达：This can help employees accomplish their leader’s tasks more effectively.
关键概括：任务完成 (task accomplishment)
我认为父母应该在孩子心情不好或动力不足时给予鼓励。
英文表达：I think parents should encourage their children when they are feeling low or unmotivated.
关键概括：父母鼓励 (parental encouragement)
高昂的情绪和积极的动力可以帮助孩子长期坚持做一件事情。
英文表达：Positive mood and high motivation can help children persist in doing something for a long time.
关键概括：坚持/动力 (motivation & persistence)
父母的鼓励应经过深思熟虑，避免过于强硬或造成压力。
英文表达：Parental encouragement should be thoughtful, avoiding being too forceful or stressful.
关键概括：正面鼓励 (positive encouragement)
有些人比其他人更擅长说服，这取决于他们的性格和经验。
英文表达：Some people are better than others at persuading, depending on their personality and experience.
关键概括：说服能力 (persuasion skill)
例如老师和医生通常需要耐心地说服学生或患者完成学习或服药。
英文表达：For example, teachers and doctors often need to patiently persuade students or patients to study or take their medicine.
关键概括：职业说服 (professional persuasion)
我认为孩子不应该完全按照父母的要求去做所有事情，尤其是在他们长大后。
英文表达：I think children should not follow everything their parents ask them to do, especially when they grow older.
关键概括：独立思考 (independent thinking)
父母的经验有用，但时代不同，方法未必适合年轻一代。
英文表达：Parents’ experience can be useful, but the methods may not suit the younger generation due to different times.
关键概括：代际差异 (generation gap)
雇主可以通过提升员工的动力和改善心情来鼓励他们。
英文表达：Employers can encourage their staff by boosting motivation and improving mood.
关键概括：员工激励 (staff encouragement)
这不仅能帮助员工完成公司任务，还能缓解他们的压力和焦虑。
英文表达：This not only helps employees complete company tasks but also relieves their stress and anxiety.
关键概括：减压/完成任务 (stress relief & task completion)
雇主可以通过营造良好的工作环境和认可员工表现来增强动力。
英文表达：Employers can also boost motivation by creating a good work environment and recognizing employees’ performance.
关键概括：工作环境/认可 (work environment & recognition)
提供适当的奖励和福利也能让员工更加积极投入工作。
英文表达：Providing appropriate rewards and benefits can also make employees more engaged.
关键概括：奖励福利 (rewards & benefits)
我认为领导者可以通过耐心的方式鼓励员工，维持积极的氛围，并帮助他们有效完成任务。
英文：I think leaders can encourage their employees in a patient way to maintain a positive atmosphere and help them complete tasks effectively.
关键概括：领导/耐心 (leaders & patience)
父母应该在孩子情绪低落或缺乏动力时鼓励他们，以保持学习或玩耍的高积极性。
英文：Parents should encourage their children when they are feeling depressed or unmotivated to maintain high motivation for learning or playing.
关键概括：父母/鼓励 (parents & encouragement)
鼓励应该经过深思且积极，否则过于强制或紧迫的建议会让孩子感到疲惫且无效。
英文：The encouragement should be thoughtful and positive, otherwise intensive or forced advice may tire children and have no effect.
关键概括：鼓励/正向 (encouragement & positive)
有些人天生更擅长说服他人，受性格或经验影响，例如教师和医生，他们经常练习说服。
我有一个朋友自学了视频剪辑，并经常在作品里加入自己挑选的音乐。
英文表达：I have a friend who taught himself video editing and often adds music he selects to his projects.
关键概括：自学/音乐创意 (self-taught & music creativity)
他的计划能力很强，每次制作视频之前都会先列好详细的步骤。
英文表达：He is very organized and always makes a detailed plan before creating a video.
关键概括：计划能力 (planning skill)
我很钦佩他的创造力，因为他总能把普通素材变得有趣。
英文表达：I really admire his creativity because he can make ordinary footage interesting.
关键概括：创造力 (creativity)
他经常分享自己的作品，我也从中学到了很多技巧和灵感。
英文表达：He often shares his work, and I have learned many skills and gained inspiration from it.
关键概括：学习/启发 (learning & inspiration)
我在拍摄短视频时曾发生过一次糗事，最后不小心被朋友录了下来。
英文表达：While filming a short video, I had an embarrassing moment that my friend accidentally recorded.
关键概括：糗事/拍摄 (embarrassing moment & filming)
这个视频后来被我们分享到社交媒体上，引来了很多笑声和评论。
英文表达：Later, we shared the video on social media, which attracted a lot of laughs and comments.
关键概括：社交媒体/互动 (social media & interaction)
这种经历让我觉得有趣，也让我不再那么在意小失误。
英文表达：This experience was fun and made me less worried about small mistakes.
关键概括：趣味/放松 (fun & relaxed)
我喜欢记录日常小事情，因为以后回看会觉得很有趣。
英文表达：I enjoy recording small daily events because looking back later can be very amusing.
关键概括：日常/回顾 (daily life & reflection)
过年整理家里的老盘子时，我不小心摔碎了一只十几年的盘子。
英文表达：While cleaning the old plates at home for the New Year, I accidentally broke a plate that had been there for over ten years.
关键概括：意外/老物件 (accident & old item)
我立刻感到很内疚，但家人只是笑着安慰我说没关系。
英文表达：I immediately felt guilty, but my family just smiled and reassured me that it was okay.
关键概括：内疚/安慰 (guilt & reassurance)
这件事让我意识到，整理家里时要更加小心。
英文表达：This incident made me realize that I should be more careful when organizing the house.
关键概括：教训/注意 (lesson & caution)
过年购物时，我没仔细核对清单，家人买错了东西，后来向我道歉。
英文表达：During New Year shopping, I didn’t carefully check the list, and my family bought the wrong items. Later, they apologized to me.
关键概括：购物误会/道歉 (shopping mistake & apology)
我原本有点生气，但看到他们诚恳地道歉，我很快原谅了他们。
英文表达：I was a bit upset at first, but seeing their sincere apology, I forgave them quickly.
关键概括：原谅/诚恳 (forgiveness & sincerity)
这个经历让我明白，沟通和理解比责备更重要。
英文表达：This experience made me realize that communication and understanding are more important than blaming.
关键概括：反思/理解 (reflection & understanding)
